Tips and Tricks

  1. A 40L backpack is the maximum allowable carry-on.
  2. Use compression packing cubes; I like Eagle Creek and Thule. Place all pants in a large packing cube, shirts in a medium packing cube, and shorts in a small packing cube.
  3. Pack light; you will for sure buy clothes on the way.
  4. Organize electronics separately so cabling does not get messy.
